How to download

  1. Ensure you have a working PC or gaming console
    • Windows 10-11 is required to run the game
    • Next gen consoles: PS5 or Xbox
    • GPU 4060 Series or greater
    • CPU: Intel series 7 or higher
  2. Head over to the Official Mar site and in the top right click the purple download icon. If you're on console head over to the app store and search "Mar games" and proceed to download the app
  3. Once installed load up the game and login to your EA account
  4. Once logged in you should be at the main menu successfully and you're ready to play!
